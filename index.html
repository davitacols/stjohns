A Warm Welcome

Arrive as you are. A smile and a cuppa will be waiting.

Our Sunday morning service is relaxed and welcoming, with Junior Church for children and refreshments for everyone afterwards. You'll be greeted at the door, helped to find a seat, and given a printed sheet so nothing is assumed.

  • Quiet, traditional Holy Communion at 8:00am.
  • Main Sunday gathering at 10:00am with music, sermon, and children's groups.
  • No special dress code, and no need to know the words in advance.
  • Music, prayer, Scripture, and conversation rooted in the life of the parish.
