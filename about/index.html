Our Story

Serving the parish since 1911.

St John the Evangelist Church has stood at the heart of Friern Barnet since 1911. Built in the Gothic Revival style, our Grade II listed building is a landmark of the area and a place of beauty, prayer, and community life.

We are part of the Diocese of London and the Church of England, committed to the historic Christian faith while seeking to engage faithfully and creatively with the world around us.

What We Believe

Historic Christian faith, lived with warmth and hospitality.

Our congregation is wonderfully diverse: people of many ages, backgrounds, and nationalities, united by faith in Jesus Christ and a shared commitment to the life of the parish.
