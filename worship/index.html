Worship at St John's

Come and worship with us, whatever stage of faith you're in.

Our worship is rooted in Scripture, shaped by prayer and sacrament, and held in a spirit of warmth and hospitality. We would love to welcome you personally.

Our main weekly gathering, with music, sermon, Junior Church, and refreshments afterwards. This is the best place to step into the shared life of the parish.

What To Expect

Nothing is assumed, and no one is left to figure it out alone.

You'll be greeted at the door and helped to find a seat. A printed service sheet guides you through the service, so whether church feels deeply familiar or brand new, you can relax and take part at your own pace.

Come as you are

Whatever you're comfortable in. You don't need to dress up, know the words, or have all the answers.

Children are welcome

Junior Church meets during the 10:00am service for age-appropriate activities, stories, and prayer.

Refreshments afterwards

After the service everyone is invited for tea, coffee, and conversation. This is where community happens.

Music In Worship

A wide, generous musical life.

Music is central to worship at St John's. Our choir and music team lead singing at the 10:00am service, drawing on traditional hymns and contemporary songs. New voices and musicians are always warmly welcome.

Choir at 10:00am Wednesday rehearsals Traditional and contemporary song
First Visit

A gentle first step if you're unsure.

If you'd like someone to look out for you, answer a question beforehand, or point you in the right direction on arrival, contact us and we'll gladly help.
