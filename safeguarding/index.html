Reporting A Concern

If you're worried, please raise it.

If you are concerned about the welfare of a child or adult, please do not hesitate to raise your concern even if you are not certain. It is always better to raise a concern that turns out to be unfounded than to stay silent.

1. In an emergency

If you believe someone is in immediate danger, call 999 first.

2. Contact the safeguarding officer

Email safeguarding@stjohnsfriernbarnet.org or call 020 8361 3081.

3. Children's Services

For non-emergency concerns involving children, you can also contact Barnet Children's Services on 020 8359 4066.
