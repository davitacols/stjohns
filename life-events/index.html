Baptisms & Christenings

Celebrate new life and welcome someone into the Christian family.

Baptism is a joyful celebration of welcome for babies, children, and adults alike. We baptise throughout the year and love to make each service meaningful, personal, and rooted in the support of the wider church family.

  • Baptisms usually take place within a Sunday service.
  • Preparation meetings are offered to help you and your family.
  • We welcome baptism enquiries for people of all ages.

Weddings

A meaningful place to begin married life together.

Getting married at St John's is a beautiful and hopeful way to begin your life together. We work with you to create a service that truly reflects your story.

  • We welcome couples who meet the legal requirements to marry here.
  • A wide choice of music, readings, and vows can be explored with you.
  • Marriage preparation is offered and encouraged.
  • We can recommend local suppliers for flowers, photography, and more.

Funerals & Memorial Services

Compassionate care at a time of loss.

At a time of loss, we walk alongside you. We offer funerals and memorial services that honour the life of your loved one and bring comfort to those who grieve.

  • We conduct funerals at church, crematorium, and graveside.
  • We work with local funeral directors.
  • Memorial services can be arranged separately.
  • Ongoing pastoral support is available after bereavement.
