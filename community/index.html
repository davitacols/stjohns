Community Life

The heartbeat of our life together.

St John's is more than Sunday. We are woven into the fabric of Friern Barnet through friendship, service, prayer, support, and shared life throughout the week.

Regular rhythm Playgroup, home groups, choir, school links, and pastoral care.
For the parish A community hub in the heart of Friern Barnet.
Families

Little Sparrows Playgroup

A friendly playgroup for babies, toddlers, and their carers with toys, crafts, songs, and a chance to meet other parents. Term-time Thursdays, 9:30-11:30am.

Friendship & Prayer

Home Groups

Small groups meeting in homes across the parish for Bible study, prayer, and friendship. A good way to go deeper in faith and community.

Care

Pastoral Care

Our pastoral team offers support for people going through difficult times, including bereavement, illness, isolation, and seasons of change.

Schools

Schools Partnership

We maintain close links with local primary schools through collective worship, church visits, and support for RE teaching.

Service

Mission & Outreach

We support local and international mission partners and engage in regular community outreach within Friern Barnet and beyond.

Music

Choir & Music

Our choir sings at the 10:00am service and for special occasions. New voices of all abilities are warmly welcomed.

Shared Life

Open, welcoming, and outward-looking.

We are called to serve our community and world, not simply to maintain our own life together. That means making room for friendship, companionship, and practical support as much as worship.

  • Community events and gatherings in the church and halls.
  • Supportive relationships across generations and backgrounds.
  • A parish life where prayer and hospitality belong together.
